Mantras for Honouring Caraṇāmṛta

Sanskrit term Caraṇāmṛta meaning “the nectar of the feet.” It refers to the sacred water or liquid that has been used to wash the feet of a deity, spiritual master, or saint. It is considered highly purifying and spiritually potent, imbued with divine blessings.

General mantra for honouring caraṇāmṛta

akāla-mṛtyu-haraṇaṁ

sarva-vyādhi-vināśanam

viṣṇoḥ pādodakaṁ pītvā

śirasā dhārayāmy aham

I drink the water from the lotus feet of Śrī Viṣṇu, which destroys all diseases and removes the chance of untimely death, and I also take that water on my head.

chaitanya academy • arcana

Add tulasī to all the ingredients for abhisheka

In the pujari room (behind the closed curtains of the altar), change the Deities into gamchas and put on day garlands.

Purification of the paraphernalia

dravya-śuddhi

Lightly sprinkle water from the ācaman on the parafernalis and repeat:

oṁ astrāya phaṭ

Preparation of Snana vedi

Wash and decorate the Snāna vedi, the place of bathing well with cloths, fruits and flowers.

Draw a Goloka yantra at the place where the Deities will be bathed and offer flowers and Tulasī to the place where the Deities will stand.

Bell worship

ghanta-puja

Offer flower petals with sandalwood paste to the bell ringer and repeat:

ete gandha-puspe oṁ jaya-dhuani-mantra-matāh svāhā

Attach the flower petal to the body of the bell with a chandana.

Then ring the bell lightly with the left hand and place it on the plate, while thinking of the bell as a servant of the Lord.

chaitanya academy • arcana

Śṛṅgāra ārati Greeting the Deities

7:10 a.m. The small Deities and Tulasī are placed on the altar.

Clean floor. Light and offer incense. Offer mirror.

7:15 a.m. Blow conch, open curtain. Offer cāmara and peacock fan (not in winter).

7:50 a.m. Make up plate of: Daily sweet 2 piece; halava; kitchari; subji; puris 2 each; milk; water; fruit salad.

Breakfast Offering

8:00 a.m. Close Deity curtain, use small bell during class. Clean tables and floor, set out sitting mats, bring in offering. Remove Kṛṣṇa’s flute.

8:10 a.m. Breakfast Offering (follow the bhoga offering procedure)

8:25 a.m. Offer Deities ācamanīyaṁ. Remove plates, pick up asana, replace Kṛṣṇa’s flute, bring in incense and flower plate for ārati.

8:30 a.m. Dhūpa-ārati. Open Deity curtain. Offer incense; flowers; cāmara and peacock fan (not in winter) 10 mins. Clean altar.

Rāja-bhoga Offering and ārati

11:40 a.m. Pūjārī makes bhoga plates.

11:50 a.m. Close Deity curtain, wipe tables with wet cloth. Clean floor.

Remove flute, place asanas, put flower petals on them. Bring in offering plates.

11:55 a.m. Make offering bhoga (see offering procedure).

Make up full ārati trays: incense; camphor lamp, ghee lamp; water; handkerchief; flowers; cāmara and peacock fan (not in winter).

12:25 a.m. Enter altar knocking the door, remove plates, replace flute.

12:30 noon Blow conch and open curtains.

Offer full ārati (see ārati procedure)

12:55 p.m. Finish ārati

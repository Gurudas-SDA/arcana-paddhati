chaitanya academy • arcana

The pūjārī should arrive no later than 3:20 a.m.

Perform Viśeṣa-ācamana (full ācamana)

Preparations:

Make up 1 tray with:

Sweets, sweet rice, cup water, cup milk

Note: place tulasī on each preparation.

Make up āratī tray with:

Incense, ghee lamp, flowers, handkerchief, water, cotton stick for perfume oil.

3:40 a.m. Waking the Lord

Prayers to be chanted upon entering the Deity room

Remaining outside the Deity room, ring the bell.

Reciting following verses, pray to the Lord to awaken.

so ’sāv adabhra-karuṇo bhagavān vivṛddha-

prema-smitena nayanāmburuhaṁ vijṛmbhan

utthāya viśva-vijayāya ca no viṣādaṁ

mādhvyā girāpanayatāt puruṣaḥ purāṇaḥ

Śrī Bhagavān, who is supreme and primeval, is unlimitedly merciful. I pray that He bless me with His smiling glance by opening His lotus eyes. He can uplift the entire cosmic creation and remove our grief and sorrow with the sweet words of instruction emanating from His lotus mouth. [SB 3.9.25]

deva prapannārti-hara prasādaṁ kuru keśava

avalokana-dānena bhūyo māṁ parayācyuta

Keśava, O infallible one, O remover of the distress of the surrendered souls, kindly bestow Your mercy upon me by again awarding me with Your transcendental glance.

jaya jaya kṛpamaya jagatera nātha

sarva jagatere koro śubha-dṛṣṭī-pāta

O Bhagavān! All glories, all glories unto You, O merciful Lord of the universe,

please cast Your auspicious glance upon all the worlds.

chaitanya academy • arcana

Reception

While ringing a bell, chant:

eṣa puṣpāñjali klīng gaurāya svāhā śrīng klīng rādhā-kṛṣṇābhyām namaḥ

Offer flower petals to Their Lordship’s lotus feet.

1) Āsana (seat)

idaṁ āsanam klīng gaurāya svāhā śrīng klīng rādhā-kṛṣṇābhyām namaḥ

Offer an āsana for Their Lordships to sit on. Escort Them to the place of worship with a gesture of your hands. Offer a flower dipped in candana into the snā-na-pātra for the purpose of offering an āsana.

2) Svāgata (a welcome)

svāgataṁ su-svāgatam klīng gaurāya svāhā śrīng klīng rādhā-kṛṣṇābhyām namaḥ

With joined palms welcome Their Lordships and see to Their comfort.

3) Padya (footbath)

etat pādyaṁ klīng gaurāya svāhā śrīng klīng rādhā-kṛṣṇābhyām namaḥ

Offer padya water to wash Their Lordships’ lotus feet.

4) Arghya (a symbolic offering)

idaṁ arghyam klīng gaurāya svāhā śrīng klīng rādhā-kṛṣṇābhyām namaḥ

Offer arghya to Their Lordships in Their hands so that They can sprinkle it over Their heads.

5) Ācamana (mouthwash)

idaṁ ācamanīyaṁ klīng gaurāya svāhā śrīng klīng rādhā-kṛṣṇābhyām namaḥ

Offer Their Lordships water for sipping.

most dear beloved. o goddess, You alone can bestow pure love for Śrī kṛṣṇa. Therefore, I offer my humble obeisances unto You.

Offering mahā-prasāda

After the worship of the Deity is complete, offer the mahā-prasāda and any prasādī (remnant) flowers or garlands to śrī guru and the Vaiṣṇavas by chanting the following mantras.

idaṁ mahā-prasādaṁ nirmālyādikam aing śrī gurave namaḥ

idaṁ pānīyaṁ aing śrī gurave namaḥ

idaṁ ācamanīyaṁ aing śrī gurave namaḥ

idaṁ sarvaṁ oṁ sarva-sakhībhyo namaḥ

idaṁ sarvaṁ oṁ sarva-vaiṣṇavebhyo namaḥ

idaṁ sarvaṁ oṁ śrī paurṇamāsyai namaḥ

idaṁ sarvaṁ oṁ sarva-vrajavāsibhyo namaḥ

Chant the aparādha-śodhana mantras

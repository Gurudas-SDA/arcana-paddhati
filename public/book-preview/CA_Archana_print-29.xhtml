chaitanya academy • arcana

Guru

Then invite Srī Gurudeva to his resting place with the following mantra:

āgaccha viśrāma-sthānaṁ

sva-gaṇaiḥ saha śrī-guro

aing gurave namaḥ

O Gurudeva, kindly come to your resting place along with all your associates.

Waking up the Deities

3:15 p.m. Cut and prepare fruit bhoga offering. Make up offering plate: 2 cookies; 3 bowls fruit; 1 drink; water (1 bowl honey for Balarāma);

3:35 p.m. Wake up the Deities (see morning procedure);

3:40 p.m. Make offering (see offering procedure);

3:55 p.m. Remove plate. Perform ācamana for Deities. Remove asanas, clean tables, floor, etc.;

4:00 p.m. Dhūpa-ārati:

Open curtains, offer incense; flowers; cāmara; peacock fan (not in winter);

4:10 p.m. Finish ārati, clean up, wash floor, etc.

Sandhyā-ārati and Offering

6:10 p.m. Make offering plate: 3 subjis; 4 puris; 1 halava; 1 milk; water; 2 daily sweets;

6:30 p.m. Close curtains. Set out asanas etc. for offering;

6:40 p.m. Offer bhoga and make up full ārati trays;

6:55 p.m. Remove plates. Perform ācamana for Deities. Remove asanas, clean tables, floor, etc.;

7:00 p.m. Sandhya ārati

7:25 p.m. Finish ārati.

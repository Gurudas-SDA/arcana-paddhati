chaitanya academy • arcana

Worship

11) Gandha (scented candana)

eṣa gandhaḥ klīng gaurāya svāhā śrīng klīng rādhā-kṛṣṇābhyām namaḥ

Apply candana mixed with scents, according to season, to Their Lordships’ lotus feet, hands and head;

12) Puṣpa (flowers)

etāni puṣpāni klīng gaurāya svāhā śrīng klīng rādhā-kṛṣṇābhyām namaḥ

Offer fragrant flowers or flower petals dipped in candana to Their Lordships’ lotus feet while ringing a bell. You may offer additional flowers for decoration at this time.

etat tulasī-pātram (for one leaf) or etāni tulasī-patrāṇi (for several leaves) klīṁ kṛṣṇāya namaḥ

Offer tulasī leaves and mañjarīs with candana to Kṛṣṇa’s lotus feet.

Place a tulasī leaf in Rādhā’s right hand, for Her to offer to Kṛṣṇa.

13) Dhūpa (incense)

eṣa dhūpaḥ klīng gaurāya svāhā svāhā śrīng klīng rādhā-kṛṣṇābhyām namaḥ

Offer incense.

14) Dīpa (ghee lamp)

eṣa dīpaḥ klīng gaurāya svāhā svāhā śrīng klīng rādhā-kṛṣṇābhyām namaḥ

Offer a ghee lamp.

Bathing and Dressing

Meditate on applying fragrant oil to the beautiful limbs of śrī gurudeva and then continue. While ringing the bell and reciting prayers:

(7) snāna (bath)

idaṁ snānīyaṁ aing gurave namaḥ

bathe śrī gurudeva with fragrant water poured from the conch shell into the snā-na-pātra.

Note: One can dry the limbs of śrī gurudeva by mind, or wave a cloth in front of him in ārati fashion.

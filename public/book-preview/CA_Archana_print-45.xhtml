9) Vastra (cloth)

idaṁ vastram klīng gaurāya svāhā śrīng klīng rādhā-kṛṣṇābhyām namaḥ

Offer fresh clothing to Their Lordships. (dhoti)

idaṁ ācamanīyaṁ klīng gaurāya svāhā śrīng klīng rādhā-kṛṣṇābhyām namaḥ

Offer Their Lordships water for sipping.

idaṁ upavītam klīng gaurāya svāhā klīṁ kṛṣṇāya namaḥ

Offer upavīta to Lord Caitanya and Sri Kṛṣṇa?

idaṁ tilakam klīng gaurāya svāhā klīṁ kṛṣṇāya namaḥ

Offer ūrdhva-puṇḍra tilaka to Lord Caitanya and Sri Kṛṣṇa? before putting on upper cloth

NOTE: When offering tilaka you may also chant the names of Viṣṇu, as when applying tilaka on yourself: oṁ keśavāya namaḥ for the forehead, etc.

10) Ābharaṇa (ornaments)

imāni ābharaṇāni klīng gaurāya svāhā śrīng klīng rādhā-kṛṣṇābhyām namaḥ

Offer Their Lordships ornaments and decorate Them.

idaṁ malyam klīng gaurāya svāhā śrīng klīng rādhā-kṛṣṇābhyām namaḥ

Offer flower garlands.

idaṁ kaṅkaṇam śrīng klīng rādhā-kṛṣṇābhyām namaḥ

Comb, arrange, and decorate Their hair.

Arcana procedure

After meditating in this way, chant Srī Gāndhārvikā- Giridhārī Srī Rādhā-Vinoda-Bihārījī kī jaya. First perform worship within the mind (mānasa-pūjā), and then, with external items, perform worship of the Divine Couple – in the form of the Deity. Offer each item first to Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa, then Śrīmatī Rādhārānī.

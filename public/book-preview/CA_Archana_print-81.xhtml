Prepāration for Worship (pūrvāṅga-karma)

Required Paraphernalia

To avoid interrupting the worship, see to it that the following items are present before beginning:

pañca-pātra, containing fresh water and a spoon

bathing conch shell

a small bell on a plate

Dūpa, incense

Dīpa, ghee lamp

Visarjanīya-pātra, throw-out pot

Pūjā-pātra for the deity

Snāna-pātra bathing receptacle for the deity

Pūjā-pātra for śrī gurudeva

Snāna-pātra,bathing receptacle for śrī gurudeva

Water-pot

Flowers

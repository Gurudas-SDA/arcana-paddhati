chaitanya academy • arcana

First purify yourself by performing ācaman by reciting the following mantras:

oṁ keśavāya namaḥ

oṁ nārāyaṇāya namaḥ

oṁ mādhavāya namaḥ

Makes bhoga plates.

Close Deity curtains, wipe tables with wet cloth.

Clean floor.

Remove crown and flute, place asanas, put flower petals on them.

Bring in offering plates.

Make offering bhoga.

While ringing the bell and chanting the following mantras

Invite Deities to āsanaṁ

Offer puṣpāñjali (a few flower petals) at the lotus feet of each deity

eṣa puṣpāñjaliḥ klīng gaurāya svāhā

śrīng klīng rādhā-kṛṣṇābhyāṁ namaḥ

Offer Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Śrī Śrī Rādhā Kṛṣṇa āsanaṁ (a seat)

idaṁ āsanaṁ klīng gaurāya svāhā

śrīng klīng rādhā-kṛṣṇābhyāṁ namaḥ

Offer Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Śrī Śrī Rādhā-Kṛṣṇa pādyaṁ, (washing the lotus feet of Deities)

This water is for washing the Lord’s lotus feet from pañca-pātra and offered to the Lord with a spoon whilst meditating that you are pouring it over His lotus-feet. Water is discarded into an empty container (visarjanīya-pātra). While ringing the bell and saying the mantra:

etat pādyaṁ klīng gaurāya svāhā

śrīng klīng rādhā-kṛṣṇābhyāṁ namaḥ

Offer Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Śrī Śrī Rādhā-Kṛṣṇa a ācaman (sipping water)

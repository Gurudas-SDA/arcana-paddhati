aing gurave namaḥ

I offer obeisances to my spiritual master.

aing parama-gurave namaḥ

I offer obeisances to my grand spiritual master.

aing parameṣṭhi gurave namaḥ

I offer obeisances to my great grand spiritual master.

aing śrī guru-paramparāyai namaḥ

I offer obeisances to śrī guru-paramparā.

oṁ sarva-gurūttamāya śrī kṛṣṇa-caitanyāya namaḥ

I offer obeisances to the topmost of all gurus, Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya.

Purifying Hands

kara-śuddhi

Wash your hands with water from the waterpot.

Purifying the Flowers

puṣpa-śuddhi

Sprinkle the flowers with Samānya-arghya (holy rivers) water while chanting:

oṁ astrāya phaṭ

oṁ puṣpe puṣpe mahā-puṣpe

su-puṣpe puṣpa-sambhave

puṣpe cayavākīrṇe ca

huṁ phaṭ svāhā

O flowers, O great and auspicious flowers who have appeared from a budding creeper, may you be purified.

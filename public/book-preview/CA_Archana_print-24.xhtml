Then knock on the door and enter the Deity room.

Turn on a soft light and sit down on the āsana.

Perform Sādhāraṇa-ācamana (ordinary ācamana) and, while ringing the bell, proceed to the place where Srī Gurudeva is resting. Awaken him by touching his feet and chant the following prayers.

Prayers in Deity’s room

Touching the feet of Śrī Gurudev, awaken Him by chanting:

uttiṣṭhottiṣṭha śrī guro

tyaja nidrāṁ kṛpāmaya

aing gurave namaḥ

O most merciful Gurudeva, please arise,

please arise and give up your sleep.

Then, touching the feet of Śrī Gaurānga, awaken Him by chanting:

uttiṣṭhottiṣṭha gaurāṅga

jahi nidrāṁ mahāprabho

śubha-dṛṣṭi pradānena

trailokya-maṅgalaṁ kuru

klīng gaurāya svāhā

O Gaurāṅga Mahāprabhu, please arise, please arise and give up Your sleep. Please benedict the three worlds with Your auspicious glance.

Next, while touching the lotus feet of Śrī Rādhā Krsna awaken Them by chanting:

go-gopa-gokulānanda

yaśodānanda vardhana

uttiṣṭha rādhayā sārddhaṁ

prātar āsīj jagat-pate

śrīng klīng rādhā-kṛṣṇābhyāṁ namaḥ

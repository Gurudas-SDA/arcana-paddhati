chaitanya academy • arcana

This water is for rinsing the Lord’s mouth and is offered by spoon from a pañca-pātra. The spoon should be held before the mouth of the Lord whilst the pūjārī meditates that he is offering water to the Lord for rinsing His mouth. Discard water into an empty container (visarjanīya-pātra). While ringing the bell and saying the mantra:

idaṁ ācamanīyaṁ klīng gaurāya svāhā

śrīng klīng rādhā-kṛṣṇābhyāṁ namaḥ

Place a Tulasī leaf on each dish offered. While ringing the bell and saying the mantra:

* idaṁ naivedyaṁ sa tulasī pānīya jalam klīng gaurāya svāhā

śrīng klīng rādhā-kṛṣṇābhyāṁ namaḥ

Hold the right hand over the bhoga plate, place the left hand on top of the right hand and repeat the gopāla-mantra 8 times.

Come out of the Deity room. Chant the gaura-gāyatrī ten times as well as the kāma-gāyatrī ten times and wait for some time for the Lord to take His meal.

After the Lord’s meal

After some time pujari knocks on the door and enters the Deity room.

Invite the Deities to rinse their mouths with the following mantras:

Offer water from the pañca-pātra into the throw-out pot (visarjanīya-pātra) to signify the offering of mouthwash.

idaṁ ācamanīyaṁ klīng gaurāya svāhā

śrīng klīng rādhā-kṛṣṇābhyāṁ namaḥ

Offer tambula (betel) to Śrī Śrī Rādhā-Kṛṣṇa

idaṁ tāmbūlaṁ klīng gaurāya svāhā

śrīng klīng rādhā-kṛṣṇābhyāṁ namaḥ

Massage

Remove the flower garlands from the Deities

Rub the Deities with oil - idaṁ sugandha-tailam...

Then rub the Deities with sesame paste (3/4) - idaṁ tila-udvartanam...

Wash the Deities with water and sesame paste through the sink (1/4) - idaṁ til-snaniyam...

Offer fruit garlands to the Deities - idaṁ phala-malayam...

Panchamrta snana

Milk - idaṁ ksira-snaniyam...

Yogurt - idaṁ dadhi-snaniyam...

Ghee - idaṁ ghrta-snaniyam...

Honey - idaṁ madhu-snaniyam...

Syrup - idaṁ sita-snaniyam...

Powders

Using a cow’s tail as a brush, apply a mixture of powders on the Deities.

Clean the Deities with a brush

Wash the Deities with plain water - idaṁ jala-snaniyam...

Phalam

Bathe the Deities with fruit juices (preferably 5 sweet kinds) - idaṁ phala-snaniyam...

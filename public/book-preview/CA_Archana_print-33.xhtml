chaitanya academy • arcana

Outside the Deity room (Pujari room) perform ācamana and offer obeisances to your spiritual master.

Pujari collects the necessary paraphernalia for the worship of Deities.

Required Paraphernalia

To avoid interrupting the worship, see to it that the following items are present before beginning:

a small bell on a plate;

a Pañca-pātra containing fresh water and a spoon;

a waterpot with a cover and a spout, filled with water;

a Visarjanīya-pātra (throw-out pot);

powdered Gopī-candana mixed with lemon juice (for brass Deities);

Taila (fragrant oil);

a water-conch on a stand;

a Snāna-pātra (bathing receptacle);

a receptacle for caraṇāmṛta;

outfits and pins;

ornaments and blutack;

Gandha (sandalwood paste);

flowers and flower garlands;

tulasī leaves;

Padya-pātra, Argya-pātra, Ācamana-pātra (one for the Lord and one for you), Madhuparka-pātra;

incense and 1 ghee lamps;

Bhoga plate preparations, naivedya - sweets; fruits and water

a picture of your spiritual master and a receptacle for offerings;

Flower Garlands 2 garlands for Rādhā-Śyāmasundara already made, 1 garland for Lord Caitanya already made;

Bathing towels

Gamchas for Deities

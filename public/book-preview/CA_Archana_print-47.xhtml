16) Praṇāma (obeisances)

Offer respect, glorification and obeisances.

Silently chant the gopāla-mantra and the kāma-gāyatrī ten times each, and thenn chant the rādhā-gāyatrī ten times

Śrī rādhā-mantra: rāṁ rādhāyai namaḥ

Śrī rādhā-gāyatrī:

rāṁ rādhikāyai vidmahe prema-rūpāyai dhīmahi tan no rādhā praco-dayāt

he kṛṣṇa karuṇā-sindho

dīna-bandho jagat-pate

gopeśa gopikā-kānta

rādhā-kānta namo ’stu te

I offer praṇāma to Śrī kṛṣṇa, who is an ocean of mercy, the friend of the distressed and the source of creation. He is the master of the gopas, and the lover of the gopīs, headed by Śrīmatī Rādhikā.

tapta-kāṣcana-gaurāṅgi

rādhe vṛndāvaneśvari

vṛṣabhānu-sute devi

praṇamāmi hari-priye

I offer praṇāma to Śrīmatī Rādhikā, whose complexion is like molten gold, and who is the queen of Vṛndāvana. She is the daughter of Vṛṣabhānu mahārāja and is very dear to Śrī Hari.

mahābhāva-svarūpā tvaṁ

kṛṣṇa-priyā-varīyasi

prema-bhakti-prade devi

rādhike tvāṁ namāmy aham

O Śrīmatī Rādhikā, because You are the embodiment of mahābhāva, You are Śrī kṛṣṇa’s
